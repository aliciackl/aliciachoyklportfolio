Fashion/Textile Industry in Ho Chi Minh City – A Transdisciplinary Design Exercise

Our team founded a fictional consulting startup, Startup Consulting Co., specializing in process and logistics optimization within the fashion and textile industry in Ho Chi Minh City.

We were engaged by PASSII Enterprise to address inefficiencies in their operations during a 5-day design thinking sprint. Our goal was to streamline their internal workflows and measurement processes using digital solutions.

Focusing on automation, we developed a prototype called MetricX—a tool designed to enhance the measurement and listing process of fashion items using image capture and AI-powered precision.

MetricX

MetricX is a software system developed to help PASSII automate the process of measuring garments. Users mark key measurement points on an image of the clothing, and the system calculates the dimensions automatically. These measurements are then logged into a database, reducing manual work and improving consistency.

We also explored geometric design inspirations through sketches involving lines, waveforms, and modular patterns, laying the foundation for a future physical product iteration.
