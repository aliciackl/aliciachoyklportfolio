Work From Home

The pandemic has forced us to re-evaluate the role of our homes in our lives. As we spent more time at home, our domestic spaces had to function as offices, schools, playgrounds, gyms, restaurants, and cinemas.

In this project, I created a narrative using floor plan drawings and produced a vector drawing and an animation to tell the story based on those drawings.

Final Product

Static Drawing

Using Rhino7, I learned to create static drawings using lines, curves, and other tools available in the software. This project recreates three scenarios involving my dog, Truffle, that occurred during the COVID-19 lockdown.

The narrative is told from Truffle’s perspective — hence the title, "A Day in Truff’s Life."

Animation

Using Adobe Photoshop, I created a frame-by-frame animation by capturing different stages of the drawing. The animation brings each scenario to life in a playful and engaging way.

Skills Learnt

  • Narrative storytelling through spatial design
  • Vector drawing using Rhino7
  • Frame-by-frame animation in Photoshop
  • Perspective-based character framing
