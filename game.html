The SUTDent Life Simulator

For this project, our team was tasked with creating a Python-based application or game that addresses a real-world problem and includes, at minimum, a text-based user interface.

We chose to focus on time management challenges faced by SUTD students. Our solution was to develop a game that encourages players to practice and improve their time management skills in a simulated environment.
