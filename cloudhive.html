Cloudhive

Co-Designing with AI

Overview

Cloudhive is a speculative design project reimagining the iconic "Cloud Bookcase" by Ronan & Erwan Bouroullec for a future where humans and bees cohabit domestic spaces. Inspired by the original form, the design introduces modular elements resembling honeycomb structures that function both as bookshelves and habitats for pollinators.

Design Process

We began with a contextual analysis of the original Cloud Bookcase’s historical relevance and impact. Next, we envisioned a speculative future where bees and humans share living environments. Sketching iterations explored how bee-friendly features could be integrated into the modular bookcase structure.

AI Integration

We used PromeAI and ChatGPT to ideate and visualize design alternatives in a collaborative workflow with AI. This helped us experiment with form, function, and aesthetics quickly and iteratively.

Final Outcome

The result is Cloudhive — a modular system that functions both as a bookcase and a bee habitat. Each module is designed to be easily assembled, rearranged, and adapted to the user’s space and sustainability goals.
