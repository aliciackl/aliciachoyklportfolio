Network of Rooms Within a City

In this project, I developed a 3D model of my room based on my floorplan. Starting from the domestic scale, I zoomed in to explore a detailed object and zoomed out to position my room within a broader urban context.

For the zoom-in detail, I modeled a camera and generated variations using AI tools. For the zoom-out, I created an axonometric drawing of my apartment block to contextualize my room within its larger environment.

Block Axonometric

Using Rhinoceros7, I created a 1:500 axonometric drawing of my block with my room highlighted. The drawing includes building forms, openings, sidewalk outlines, and nearby roads. Shading was applied to show the adjacent building obscuring the base of my block.

Room Isometric

I modeled my room in 3D using Rhinoceros7, translating its floorplan into a full spatial representation.

Object Axonometric and AI Variations

I chose my camera as the object of focus. First, I created a detailed axonometric drawing of the camera. Then, using PromeAI, I generated multiple variations exploring different materials, aesthetics, and conceptual forms.
